How much did the increase in gas prices cost you?

By Lara Heard

Americans are currently spending big bucks on their commutes and monthly gas and electric bills. The culprit: increased natural gas and gasoline prices.

In New York, the average cost per gallon of gasoline leaped from $2.61 a gallon in February 2021 to $3.72 a gallon in February of the following year. The price gains are beginning to take their toll-- according to a March Reuters/Ipsos poll, 54% of American adults would cut spending on eating out and 49% would lower other entertainment spending if gas prices reached $6 to $7 a gallon.
